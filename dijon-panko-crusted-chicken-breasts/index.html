Dijon Panko Crusted Chicken Breasts
 
Save Print
Prep time
5 mins
Cook time
20 mins
Total time
25 mins
 
Dijon Panko Crusted Chicken Breasts are a 30 minute, 5-ingredient meal with minimal prep time. A perfect meal for a busy night that your whole family will love!
Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients
  • • 1½ cups panko crumbs
  • • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • • 4 (6 oz) boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • • 6 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • • 2 garlic cloves, minced
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 450º. Place a silpat on a baking sheet or coat baking sheet with tin foil and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Pour panko crumbs and Italian seasoning onto a large plate and mix together until combined.
  3. In a small bowl, mix garlic and mustard together. Rub mustard mixture evenly over each chicken breast. Roll each chicken breast in the panko mixture until coated, then set on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Let the chicken rest 3-5 minutes before serving.
