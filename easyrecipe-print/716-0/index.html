Asparagus, Pear, and Bacon Flatbread
Prep time:  15 mins
Cook time:  15 mins
Total time:  30 mins
Serves: Makes one pizza
 
Asparagus, Pear, and Bacon Flatbread is works equally wells as an appetizer or meal with a perfect marriage of sweet and salty ingredients.
Ingredients
  • • your favorite pizza dough (homemade or store bought)
  • • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • • 1 tablespoon honey
  • • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • • ¼ teaspoon horseradish
  • • ½ pear, thinly sliced
  • • ¼ cup asparagus, thinly sliced
  • • 3 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella or torn fresh mozzarella
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 425º.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, sesame oil, honey, garlic, poppy seeds, and horseradish. Stir throughly until combined.
  3. Spread the sauce evenly over the pizza dough. Place the pear, asparagus, and bacon on top of the sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, or until pizza appears done. Let cool a bit and slice into squares and eat.
