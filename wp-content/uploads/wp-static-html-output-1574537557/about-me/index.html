About

Hey there!

Welcome to Sweet Little Kitchen! My name is Alexa. I love to create (and eat!) new recipes. I believe in always eating vegetables and sometimes saving room for a decadent dessert. I hope that Sweet Little Kitchen helps you discover recipes that are unique, healthy, and simple.

I work for a nonprofit that provides case management services to survivors of domestic minor sex trafficking. Currently, I’m working on developing recommendations for best practices in residential care for trafficked youth. I absolutely love being at the forefront of this growing field, and hope it will inform practices of child-serving organizations and public policy decisions for years to come. Besides cooking and working, some of my hobbies include strategic board games, hiking, and watching movies with at least an 85% rating from critics on Rotten Tomatoes.

My husband Drew is my faithful taste-tester and tech support extraordinaire. He is a web developer who loves functional programming. And he’s the movie snob who convinced me to always check a movie’s critical reviews before watching it. We met at Georgia Tech through our campus ministry in 2010 and got married on a rainy day in May 2014.

I love hearing from my readers (seriously, it makes my day). Feel free to comment on a post or shoot me an email if you have any questions. And thanks for checking out Sweet Little Kitchen!
