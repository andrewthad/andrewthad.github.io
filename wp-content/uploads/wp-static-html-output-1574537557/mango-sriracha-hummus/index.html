Mango Sriracha Hummus

Mango Sriracha Hummus is a unique and simple hummus recipe!

Makes approximately 2 cups
Ingredients:
1/4 cup tahini,
1 cup mango, diced (should be about an entire medium-sized mango),
2 tbsp olive oil,
3 tsp sriracha,
1/2 tsp salt,
2 tbsp water,
1 15-oz can of chickpeas, drained.
Instructions:

Place the tahini and diced mango in a food processor. Blend until it forms a chunky paste. Add the olive oil, sriracha, salt, and garlic to the mix. Blend until smooth. Add the chickpeas and water. Blend until it reaches desired consistency. Add an extra tbsp of water if needed. Serve with pita chips, pita bread, pretzels, carrot sticks . . . whatever your heart desires
